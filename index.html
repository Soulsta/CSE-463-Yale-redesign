Graduate Programs

World-class education in the visual arts

Painting & Printmaking

Explore traditional and contemporary approaches to painting and printmaking in our state-of-the-art studios. Work alongside renowned faculty and visiting artists.

Graphic Design

Push the boundaries of visual communication through experimental typography, interactive media, and critical design practices.

Photography

Master both analog and digital photography techniques while developing your unique artistic vision and critical perspective.

Sculpture

Work with diverse materials and processes, from traditional bronze casting to cutting-edge digital fabrication technologies.

Film/Video

Create compelling moving images through narrative, experimental, and documentary approaches in our professional production facilities.

Interdisciplinary

Combine multiple mediums and approaches to create innovative works that challenge traditional boundaries in contemporary art.
